About ACLEDA Bank

ACLEDA Bank is one of the leading commercial banks in the Kingdom of Cambodia. Since its establishment as a small loan project in 1993, it has grown rapidly and now operates an extensive network of branches and ATMs throughout Cambodia. The bank provides a wide range of financial services to both individual and corporate clients, contributing to Cambodia's economic development. ACLEDA Bank is highly regarded both domestically and internationally for its sound management and customer-centric services.
